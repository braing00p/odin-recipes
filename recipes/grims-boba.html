Ingredients:

For the Base:

  • 2 cups Midnight-Brewed Tea (steeped in moonlight)
  • 1/2 cup Phantom Fog Milk (coconut milk infused with spectral essence)
  • 2 tbsp Void Sugar Syrup (or until properly cursed)
  • 1/4 cup Ectoplasm Cream (for the swirls)
  • 1 cup Assorted Soul-Popping Skull Boba (choose your spirits wisely)

Skull Boba Flavours:

  • Spirit Berry (trapped souls of summer berries)

  • Lingering Lychee (harvested by friendly spirits)

Instructions:

  1. Brew tea under a moonlit sky (or your kitchen light, we're not picky) until it turns the color of a starless night.
  2. In your ceremonial shaker (or regular cup), combine the tea with Void Sugar Syrup while chanting your favorite incantations.
  3. Add Phantom Fog Milk until the mixture swirls like morning mist in a graveyard.
  4. Carefully place your chosen Soul-Popping Skull Boba into a haunted vessel (any cup will do, but skull-shaped is preferred).
  5. Pour the tea mixture over Shadow Ice, watching as the Ectoplasm Cream creates ethereal swirls.
  6. Insert your Spirit Straw (extra wide for proper skull transportation).
